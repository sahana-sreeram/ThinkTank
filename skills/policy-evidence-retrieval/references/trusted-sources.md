# Trusted Policy Evidence Sources

## Tier 1 — Peer Reviewed / Government Primary
- **OpenAlex** https://api.openalex.org/works?search={query}&mailto=you@example.com
- **BTS** https://www.bts.gov/browse-statistical-products-and-data
- **US DOT Open Data** https://data.transportation.gov (Socrata API)
- **EPA AQS** https://aqs.epa.gov/aqsweb/documents/data_api.html
- **Census ACS** https://api.census.gov/data

## Tier 2 — Regional / Municipal
- **MBTA Developer API** https://api-v3.mbta.com
- **MassDOT** https://www.mass.gov/orgs/massdot-office-of-transportation-planning
- **City of Boston Open Data** https://data.boston.gov

## Tier 3 — Policy Research Orgs (credibility_score: 0.7)
- **VTPI (Todd Litman)** https://vtpi.org — most cited in transportation demand management
- **MAPC** https://www.mapc.org/data-services

## Do Not Use
- Wikipedia, news articles, blog posts as primary citations
- Any source without a clearly identified author or organization
